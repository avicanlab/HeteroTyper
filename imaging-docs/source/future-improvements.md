# Additional Information

## Future Improvements

Several areas offer room for improvement in future iterations:

- **Lighting**: Completing the LED strip installation to cover all rows of plate
  windows (rather than every second row) would further improve illumination
  uniformity.
- **Arduino firmware**: The current shutter timing parameters (settling delay, pulse
  duration, inter-cycle interval) are hard-coded in firmware. Exposing these via a
  simple serial interface or hardware potentiometers would improve usability.
- **Camera upgrade**: A higher-resolution camera with a telephoto or macro lens and a
  smaller crop factor than the GoPro could improve the detail resolvable in individual
  bacterial colonies, at higher future budget.
